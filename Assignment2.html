FAVORITE STAR WARS MOVIES


1. Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith

This film is my favorite of the list. Finally seeing the rise and fall of the main character Anakin Skywalker into Darth Vader. Known as the climax of the saga it blended both the characracters and story dramatization perfectly with impacting action and suspence.


2. Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

Personally this film is my favorite side saga story. Delving deep into the plot of the Original Trilogy, the Force, and how the Galactic Empire and Rebel Alliance operated..


3. Star Wars Episode V: Empire Strikes Back

My favorite of the Original trilogy. Continues the journey of Luke Skywalker training to become a Jedi Knight under Imperial rule facing trials and tribulations when finally confronting his father, Darth Vader


4. Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope

Director George Lucas's legacy began with this film, back in 1977 when there were many doubts of its sucess, the amazing space opera would become one of the most iconic films in history, setting the bar for furture SciFi films.


5. Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace

After almost more than a decade in waiting The original Trilogy takes it back with this film. Highly controversial yet incredibly original and pioneered many feats in CGI and sound design. Favorite scenes being the Podracing event and the Duel of The Fates with Darth Maul, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Qui-Gon Jinn.
